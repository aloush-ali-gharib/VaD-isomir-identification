## 📁 Repository Structure

```text
VaD-miRNA-Collaborative/
│
├── core_pipeline/           # isomiR extraction and frequency merging
├── differential_expression/ # DESeq2 workflows for mRNA & miRNA
├── interaction_prediction/  # Miranda and dMiso pair generation & orchestration
├── functional_analysis/     # Pathway enrichment & visualization
├── docs/                    # Methodology overviews
└── README.md
```

- [`core_pipeline/`](./core_pipeline/) — Specialized Python scripts for capturing non-canonical 5' shifts in miRNAs.
- [`differential_expression/`](./differential_expression/) — Robust DESeq2 framework incorporating `apeglm` LFC shrinkage.
- [`functional_analysis/`](./functional_analysis/) — High-fidelity R-plots for GO, KEGG, and Reactome clusters.

---

## 💡 Why isomiR Dynamics?

The engineering within this repo solves the **"Isomer Resolution Problem"** in small RNA-seq, allowing researchers to see beyond canonical miRNAs and capture the **regulatory flips** occurring at the sub-nucleotide level during the transition to ischemic dementia.
